GOGLOBAL EDULINK is an educational consulting website, build to help student gain admission to top-tier universities around the globe.It serves as a digital link, connecting international students with study opportunities in place like SPAIN,UK,CANADA,CHINA,INDIA,AUSTRALIA,SINGAPORE. This websites primarly goal is to simplify the study-abroad process, offering complete support from Start to finish. This includes everything from choosing University to handling Visa-application, travel logistic and settling in once they arrive. The website is structured around five key pages:The Home page which shows the brand with important statistics and direct instruction,The Study destinations page which vissually highlights the countries available, and the Service page, shows the full range of support offered, including global admission,visa assistance, travel, arrival help,accommodation and scholarship guidance.<img width="468" height="275" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7261baa7-7a1a-4003-ae40-e9ef9ce6a8a2" />
